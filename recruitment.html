About the Belize Coast Guard

The Belize Coast Guard is the principal maritime law enforcement and search and rescue agency of Belize. Our mission is to protect Belize's territorial waters, enforce maritime laws, and ensure the safety of all who use our waters.

Recruitment Requirements

Basic Eligibility

  • Belizean citizen
  • Age 18-30 years
  • Minimum 4'10" height
  • Good physical condition
  • Able swimmer

Education

  • High School Diploma or equivalent
  • Pass entrance examination

Character

  • No criminal record
  • Good moral character
  • Willingness to serve
