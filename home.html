COURAGE


Our integrity is built on our character to do what is right in the face of compromise. To confront fear, pain, risk or uncertainty and to face danger, hardship or death. We are loyal to our leader and will defend our cause.

COMMITMENT


We are a professional military who seek responsibility, accept responsibility, and are committed to the successful achievement of our organizational goals. We serve that others may live. We serve with pride.

RESPECT


Respect but not fear for adversity. Respect for self and others, treating all with fairness, dignity, and compassion We foster individual opportunity and growth. We encourage creativity and innovation through education. We work as a team.

Commandant

Rear Admiral Elton Bennett


The Belize Coast Guard strategic vision is to create an apex institution highly professional, motivated, trained and resourced, capable of enforcing maritime laws and projecting sea power to the limits of our sea spaces and support maritime operations locally and regionally. Our core values are “COURAGE, COMMITMENT & RESPECT”.
