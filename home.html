Guardians of the Sea: The Belize Coast Guard


Nestled along the stunning Caribbean coastline, Belize is a paradise of turquoise waters and vibrant marine life. Protecting this natural treasure is the Belize Coast Guard (BCG), a dedicated force ensuring safety, security, and sovereignty at sea.
Since its establishment in 2005, the BCG has been the nation’s frontline defense against maritime threats—combating illegal fishing, preventing drug trafficking, and conducting life-saving search and rescue operations.
Whether patrolling the cayes, assisting distressed vessels, or collaborating with international partners, these brave men and women uphold their motto: "Utrinque Paratus, Reaedy for Aanything. From safeguarding Belize’s rich fisheries to protecting its bustling ports, the Coast Guard plays a vital role in sustaining the country’s economy and environment.
Their unwavering commitment ensures that Belize’s waters remain safe for locals and visitors alike. Next time you sail these pristine seas, remember the Belize Coast Guard—the silent sentinels watching over our blue frontier.


VISION


The Belize Coast Guard strategic vision is to create an apex institution highly professional, motivated, trained and resourced, capable of enforcing maritime laws and projecting sea power to the limits of our sea spaces and support maritime operations locally and regionally.
Our core values are "COURAGE, COMMITMENT & RESPECT".


COURAGE


Our integrity is built on our character to do what is right in the face of compromise. To confront fear, pain, risk or uncertainty and to face danger, hardship or death. We are loyal to our leader and will defend our cause.

COMMITMENT


We are a professional military who seek responsibility, accept responsibility, and are committed to the successful achievement of our organizational goals. We serve that others may live. We serve with pride.

RESPECT


Respect but not fear for adversity. Respect for self and others, treating all with fairness, dignity, and compassion We foster individual opportunity and growth. We encourage creativity and innovation through education. We work as a team.
